This tournament is the first big event of the year

It takes place in Australia during the summer,

so it’s usually very hot. Players start the season here

and the matches are fast and exciting

It’s known for a friendly atmosphere and long

dramatic games

• Location: Melbourne, Australia

• Surface: Hard court

• When: January

• Special features

• Very hot weather

• Fast, powerful games

• First Grand Slam of the year

Roland Garros

This is the toughest tournament physically.

It’s played on red clay, which makes the ball

slower and the rallies much longer.

Players have to run a lot and fight for every point.

It’s considered the most “strategic”

Slam because clay requires patience and smart play

• Location: Paris, France

• Surface: Clay court (red clay)

• When: May–June

• Special features:

• Clay is the slowest surface

• Long rallies and very physical matches

• Rafael Nadal is famous for dominating this tournament

Wimbledon is the most famous and traditional

tennis tournament. It’s played on grass,

hich makes the game faster

Players must wear all-white clothes,

and everything feels very classic and old-school.

It’s known as the “home of tennis

• Location: London, England

• Surface: Grass court

• When: July

• Special features:

•The oldest and most traditional tennis tournament

•Players must wear all white

• Fastest surface in tennis
